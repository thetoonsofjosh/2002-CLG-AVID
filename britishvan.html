British Vanity Logos

Compiled by James Fabiano

French & Saunders ("Absolutely Fabulous")
(1994-1996)

Who Are They? - Dawn French and Jennifer Saunders, two popular comediennes from Britain who created "Absolutely Fabulous." They are also well known for their skit show "French and Saunders." Saunders wrote all of the AbFab episodes and starred on it as Edina Monsoon. French appeared once on the show as a morning TV personality, and starred on "The Vicar of Dibley" as Geraldine Grainger, the title character.

Logo: The following text:

A
Saunders & French
production for the
|B| |B| |C|
C |B| |B| |C| (date)

Sometimes the spacing varies. The BBC's of course appear in the box logo.

SFX: None

Music: The end of "This Wheel's On Fire," the show's theme song.

Availability: Seen from the second series on of "Absolutely Fabulous."

Scare Factor: Low

Grant/Naylor (Red Dwarf)
(1988-)

Logo: The basic format is this.....the names:

GRANT
NAYLOR

Appear in the form of the Red Dwarf logo (with a "ring" around the names). The full logo reads, "A GRANT/NAYLOR PRODUCTION FOR BBC NORTH," with variations in the font and positions. The copyright date appears underneath of course.

SFX: Sometimes the ring flips into place before the names appear.

Music: None.

Availability: "Red Dwarf" episodes. In the later series, the production team changed, but I'm not sure how or if that affected the logo.

Scare Factor: Low

Harold Snoad (Keeping Up Appearance)
(1990-1995)

Logo: Hyacinth Bucket (pronounced "Bouquet"!) is setting her table. When she finishes, she puts a namecard down. Upon closer inspection we see the name on it is "HAROLD SNOAD." Camera backs away a bit, then the words "Produced & Directed by" appears over it. With the theme's last note, the copyright date appears under.

SFX: None

Music: The end of the "Keeping Up Appearances" theme song.

Availability: Seen on "Keeping Up Appearances" episodes.

Scare Factor: Low, Hyacinth is not her annoying self here ;-)
