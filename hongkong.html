HK/TVB International

By John Lee

HK-TVB International
(1983?-1990)

Logo: We first see a blue-colored 2-dimensional globe of the world rotating reversely than the way it should, backing away, revealing a field of stars all moving concurrently (not some moving toward the screen and others moving in the background), and then at the same time the words at the bottom say "Copyright HK-TVB International Ltd. 1983," and then a blue outlined-rectangle (square on top, small rectangle on bottom), come out, rotating out toward us until it turns upside down, and as the words I described disappear, the rectangle, now right-side up, heads toward the spinning globe, and the field of stars changes to another field of stars, and the rectangle goes up in place. 3 light beams hit the globe, and it explodes, then magically the TVB logo (blue, green, and red) appears in the square, and "HK-TVB International," in the rectangle, both in white outlines. The words "An HK-TVB International Limited Distribution" appears in the bottom, and the second field of stars stops moving. On some videos these words will appear in Chinese, since in Hong Kong, the 2 main languages are Chinese and English.

Music: First, a few laser beam sounds, then an electronic synthesizer that starts low and then gets high, then a few drum beats on the last few notes, with more laser beam sounds.

Availability: Any TVB video leading up to 1990.

Scare Factor: TBA

TVB INTERNATIONAL
(1991-1995?)

Logo: We start with a chromo shot of the words "TVB" and "INTERNATIONAL" going around a colored 3-dimensional globe rotating the same way as Logo 1, and then pan out to see both the words and the globe entirely, the words upside down on a blue background. As soon as the word "TVB" reaches the center it comes flipping down, until it's right-side up. At the same time, the blue and red shades come from the right, and the green from the left covering up the globe. The word "INTERNATIONAL" comes down too. Then the white part resembled to look like an antenna-less TV set, the part of the company's current logo, magically appears.

SFX/Cheesy Factor: TBA

Music: An few woodwinds play twice and then a few drums are added in and then they end on C Major, with a huge drum boom at the end.

Availability: Any TVB video leading up to 1995 (I think).

Scare Factor: TBA

TVBI
(1996?-)

Logo: We see the right side of a blue-colored 3-dimensional globe rotating the same way as Logos 1 and 2, with a mirror image of the logo on the right side. As soon as the Americas vanish, the letters T, V, B, and I all come around in a straight line in the direction of the globe. The globe backs away and we can see a mirror image of Europe, Australia and Asia. The TVBI name comes into the picture from the front of the screen to the left on a moving blue background, and at the same time the white TV set outline falls in and the countries change to the blue-green-red part of the logo. The words "Distributed by TVBI Company Limited," in both English AND Chinese fall into the picture. The TVBI name shines with pride and the background stops moving.

Music: Same as Logo 2.

Availability: Any TVB video from 1996 to now.
